Scientific Simposium on AI for Molecular Design & Autonomous Science

Three focused themes and tightly curated subthemes. The conference brings together leaders in generative chemistry, protein design, lab automation, LLM lab agents, and computational diagnostics.

Hybrid-friendly Hands-on sessions Industry & Academia Poster sessions

Program

Theme 1 — Generative & Predictive Models for Molecular & Protein Design

  • Complex structure & interaction prediction
  • De novo protein design
  • Generative chemistry for small molecules
  • Biologics optimization (antibodies, multispecifics)
  • Nucleic acid design (RNA / ASO / siRNA)
  • Gene editors designed by AI

Theme 2 — Autonomous “Self‑Driving” Bio Labs (SDLs) & Agentic Science

  • Robotic high‑throughput experimentation (HTE)
  • Active learning & Bayesian optimization
  • LLM lab agents & protocol generation
  • Cloud labs + data OS (ELN / LIMS / CDP)
  • Phenotypic screening at scale
  • Digital twins of cells & bioprocesses

Theme 3 — AI‑Enabled Diagnostics & Clinical Phenotyping

  • Multimodal EHR + omics foundation models
  • Computational pathology & histology
  • Liquid biopsy signal detection
